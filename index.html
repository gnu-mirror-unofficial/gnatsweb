Gnatsweb is looking for a maintainer. If you're interested, please take a look at this general information about GNU packages and being a GNU maintainer, and then email maintainers@gnu.org with a bit about your background and interest. Thanks!

Please note that maintaining this package requires assigning copyright to the FSF, and ensuring that any future contributors also execute papers, since that is what past authors and contributors have done.

Gnats itself could also use development help or a co-maintainer. Same applies.
