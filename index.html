Gnatsweb is the web interface to the GNATS defect tracking system.

Gnatsweb is currently undergoing moderization. The modernization will continue to follow the philosophy and mechanics of the GNATS defect tracking system.

To participate in its modernization, look at the Gnatsweb Savannah Project Page.

We are looking for developers who would like to participate in its modernization. We are looking for skills in:

  • Perl
  • HTML5
  • Javascript
  • Web Sockets
  • Mojolicious Framework
